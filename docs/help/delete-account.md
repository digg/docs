# What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account:

- The action cannot be undone
- Any posts you created will remain on the platform and will be anonymized unless you delete them prior to deleting your account
- Any comments you made will remain on the platform and will be anonymized unless you delete them prior to deleting your account
- Your profile picture will be replaced with a generic avatar and your name will be replaced with "Deleted".
